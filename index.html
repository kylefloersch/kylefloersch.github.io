Some of my work:

Is This Recyclable?

Is This Recyclable? Is a hackathon project that I and a couple friends worked on during the Hack The Earth 2020 competition, that I reworked into a website to showcase it. The site uses an artificial intelligence model that we trained on hundreds of images, to perform object recognition to predict and determine whether or not an uploaded image is or is not recyclable. This project was written in python and as something we scrapped together in about 24 hours, I am pretty fond of it.
